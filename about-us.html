Our Mission

The Utah Ukrainian Association (UUA) is a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization dedicated to supporting the Ukrainian community in Utah and abroad. Our mission is to raise awareness about Ukraine, provide critical humanitarian and medical aid to refugees and victims of war, and organize cultural and educational events to preserve and share our heritage.

Our History

2015

The Utah Ukrainian Association was founded to unite the local community.

February-March 2022

Responding to the full-scale invasion, UUA organized its first major rally and the impactful "Voices for Ukraine" benefit concert at the Cathedral of the Madeleine, drawing support from across the state.

February 2023

Hosted a rally at the Utah State Capitol to mark one year of war, demonstrating sustained community commitment and advocacy.

Our Impact & Key Projects

Humanitarian Aid

Coordinating the delivery of medical supplies, food, and essential items to refugees in Utah and to those affected in Ukraine.

Cultural Advocacy

Hosting concerts, art exhibitions ("Art for Peace"), and educational events to share Ukrainian culture and keep public attention on the situation.

Community Building

Providing direct support, resources, and a welcoming network to help Ukrainian refugees integrate and find community in Utah.
