Support for Refugees

Home > Support for Refugees

Housing Assistance

We provide temporary and permanent housing solutions for Ukrainian refugees arriving in Utah. Our network of host families and partner organizations helps newcomers find safe and comfortable accommodations.

Temporary Shelter

Short-term housing with host families for the first 30-90 days

Apartment Assistance

Help with finding and securing long-term rental housing

Host Family Program

Community members offering their homes to refugee families

Driver's License & Transportation

Navigating Utah's transportation system is essential for independence. We assist with driver's license applications, vehicle donations, and public transportation orientation.

License Application

Step-by-step guidance for obtaining a Utah driver's license

Study Materials

Ukrainian-language study guides for written driving tests

Transit Training

Orientation to Utah's public transportation system

Employment & Career Support

We help refugees find meaningful employment and rebuild their careers in Utah through job placement, resume assistance, and professional networking.

Resume Building

Creating effective resumes for the American job market

Job Placement

Connections with local employers seeking diverse talent

Career Counseling

Professional guidance for career transitions in the US

Education Support

Children and adults receive educational support including school enrollment assistance, tutoring, and access to higher education resources.

School Enrollment

Assistance with registering children in Utah schools

Tutoring Services

Academic support for students adjusting to new curriculum

College Guidance

Information on scholarships and university applications

English Language Learning

Language is key to integration. We offer English classes at various levels, conversation practice, and translation services.

ESL Classes

Beginner to advanced English as a Second Language courses

Conversation Practice

Weekly conversation groups with native English speakers

Translation Services

Document translation and interpreter assistance

Youth Wellness Program

Specialized support for young refugees dealing with trauma and adjustment challenges. Our program includes counseling, recreational activities, and peer support.

Counseling Services

Trauma-informed therapy with Ukrainian-speaking counselors

Recreational Activities

Sports, arts, and social events for youth integration

Peer Support Groups

Safe spaces for sharing experiences with other youth
