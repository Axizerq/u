Become a Volunteer

Join our dedicated team of volunteers and make a direct impact on the lives of Ukrainian refugees. Whether you have a few hours a week or can commit to regular assistance, your time and skills are invaluable.

Host Family

Provide temporary housing for newly arrived refugee families

  • Minimum 30-day commitment
  • Private room required
  • Background check

Transportation Assistant

Help refugees with appointments, shopping, and orientation

  • Valid driver's license
  • Own vehicle with insurance
  • Flexible schedule

Language Tutor

Teach English or help with translation services

  • Fluency in English
  • Ukrainian/Russian helpful
  • Teaching experience preferred

Food & Supply Distribution

Assist with our weekly food pantry and supply distribution

  • Thursday afternoons
  • Heavy lifting may be required
  • Team environment

Administrative Support

Help with office work, data entry, and communications

  • Computer skills required
  • Office hours (9-5 weekdays)
  • Detail-oriented

Youth Mentor

Work with refugee children and teenagers

  • Background check required
  • Experience with youth
  • After-school hours
